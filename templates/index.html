FanyaBiashara kwa sasa inapatikana Tanzania nzima(Kanda zote, mikoa yote, wilaya zote, kata na vijiji/mitaa yote, kwa Tanzania tu ) na kwa lugha ya kiingereza na kiswahili.

Mfumo huu ni bure kwa watumiaji wa kawaida(hakuna ghalama yeyote kujiunga na kutumia) , na kwa karakana/biashara kuna gharama kujiunga(kwa ajili ya usajiri wa biashara) halafu mtumiaji atachagua kifurushi cha matumizi kuanzia kifurushi cha bure hadi cha kiwango zaidi {% else %} FanyaBiashara is a web business/enterprises based platform application to be used by both business/enterprise owners and non business owners(normal users), for:
Managing enterprise’s inventory (stock management, sales, purchase, stock adjustments, accounting, production transactions and generating an overview suitable reports over time for each recorded transaction, barcode scanning capability for products and user permissions management for all enterprise users)
E-commerce for both normal users and business owners, including goods sales & purchases(ordering, packing, delivery, buyer & customer confirmation and invoicing), Items/services/enterprises searching and listing according to places.
Services (From booking, servicing, changes during service, cost with service duration to service complition & invoicing).
Digital marketing (Market enterprise/services/Goods, institutions, companies etc to users where they can acquire what is being on market) , by affordable price.

FanyaBiashara is now available in Tanzania (all zones, all regions, all districts, all wards and all Street/VillagesOnly in Tanzania ) and in English and Swahili languages.

This application is free for normal users(no any charge to join & use), and for enterprise there is charges to register the business then choosing the suitable usage plan from free to classic plans {% endif %}
